Adriano Cava

Contact Bandcamp Soundcloud Riforma NWI

Playing with frequencies and electricity to shape NoFi environments. Part of Misto Mame collective and Asile. 1/3 of Riforma screw-wave label, together with Lapo Sorride & NOT399093. Mixing for and broadcasting from 00185fm, Radio Sygma (Wide.Mesh) and Radio Blackout (Matinée XXL).

Adriano Cava is an independent researcher, composer, and sound designer based in Turin, Italy. He obtained a Master's Degree in Philosophy at University of Roma Tre and he is currently following the Master's program in Cultural Anthropology at University of Torino, working on the relation between ecology and ontology, focused on the social and technical construction of space. Since 2016, Adriano Cava plays as a solo musician and in different group projects, exploring both computer music, electroacoustic and impro free-form music. He performs around Europe and USA as Altrimenti, Adriano Cava and with Lametia trio, releasing albums on several labels such as La République des Grages, Second Sleep, Union Editions, and Mahorka. He also works as a composer for theatre, mostly with Teatro Mobile in Rome. Since 2018 Adriano is part of Misto Mame, a non linear collective spread between Rome, Turin, Bruxelles and Berlin, organising concerts between Rome and Turin. He is the co-founder of Riforma label, a catalogue of experimental electronic music, while collaborating with Radio Blackout (FM independent radio from Turin) and 00185fm (Misto Mame's web radio). He also appears as a selector and dj for different international web radios such as Radio Sygma, Radio Raheem, Kiosk, Radio Arte Mobile, Orsimanirana.
