Adriano Cava

Playing with phonography to shape NoFi soundscapes. Now presenting "mikrokosmos", a sonic pantomime aired thru a low volume multi-speaker system. He released albums on several labels such as La République des Grages, Second Sleep, Union Editions. Part of Misto Mame collective, Riforma label and Asile. Mixing for and broadcasting from 00185fm, Radio Sygma and Radio Blackout.

Adriano Cava is an independent researcher, composer, and sound maker based in Turin, Italy. Since 2016, Adriano Cava plays as a solo musician and in different group projects, exploring both computer music, electroacoustic and impro free-form music. He performs live as Altrimenti, Adriano Cava and with Lametia trio, releasing albums on several labels such as La République des Grages, Second Sleep, Union Editions etc. He also works as a composer for theatre, mostly with Teatro Mobile in Rome. Since 2018 Adriano is part of Misto Mame, a non linear collective spread between Rome, Turin, Bruxelles and Berlin, organising concerts, fest and happenings between Rome and Turin. With Lapo Sorride and NOT339093, he is the co-founder of Riforma label, a catalogue of experimental electronic music, while collaborating with Radio Blackout (FM independent radio from Turin) and 00185fm (Misto Mame's web radio). He also appears as a selector and dj for different international web radios such as Radio Sygma, Radio Raheem, Kiosk, Radio Arte Mobile, Orsimanirana.
